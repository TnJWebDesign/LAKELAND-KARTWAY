SAFETY FIRST

Your safety is our top priority. Please review our rules before arriving at the track.

AGE & HEIGHT REQUIREMENTS

  • Drivers must be at least 12 years old
  • Minimum height requirement of 4'8" (142 cm)
  • Junior karts available for younger/smaller drivers
  • Parental consent required for drivers under 18

CLOTHING REQUIREMENTS

  • Closed-toe shoes required (no sandals or flip-flops)
  • Long hair must be tied back
  • No loose clothing or scarves
  • Helmets provided and must be worn at all times

TRACK RULES

  • No bumping or intentional contact
  • Follow all flag signals from marshals
  • Maintain safe distances between karts
  • No stopping on track except in designated areas

HEALTH & CONDUCT

  • No alcohol or drugs before driving
  • Must be free from any condition that impairs driving
  • Aggressive behavior will not be tolerated
  • Follow all instructions from staff
All karts are regularly inspected and maintained to the highest safety standards
